## 2. Definitions

- **"Belief State"**: A classification (STABLE, FRAGILE, CRACKING, BROKEN) derived from observed market reaction patterns.
- **"Evidence Bundle"**: A cryptographically hashed collection of shocks, reactions, and state changes within a time window.
- **"Alert"**: A notification indicating observed belief instability, NOT a prediction or recommendation.
- **"Tile"**: A compressed heatmap segment showing order book depth over time.
- **"Replay"**: Deterministic re-processing of historical raw events for audit purposes.
- **"Reaction"**: A classified market maker response (VACUUM, SWEEP, CHASE, PULL, HOLD, DELAYED, NO_IMPACT) to a shock event.
